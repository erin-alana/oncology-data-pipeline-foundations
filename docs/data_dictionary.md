# Oncology Sample Data Dictionary

## patient_id

Description:
Unique patient identifier.

Business Importance:
Used to uniquely identify each patient record.

---

## age

Description:
Patient age at diagnosis.

Business Importance:
Frequently used in cohort selection and outcomes analysis.

---

## sex

Description:
Patient sex.

Business Importance:
Important demographic variable for reporting and analysis.

---

## primary_site

Description:
Primary cancer site.

Business Importance:
Determines disease category and downstream analytics.

---

## histology

Description:
Histology code representing tumor type.

Business Importance:
Critical for disease classification and cohort definition.

---

## stage

Description:
Cancer stage at diagnosis.

Business Importance:
Used for prognosis, outcomes analysis, and treatment planning.

Data Quality Risks:
Missing or invalid stage values can impact reporting accuracy.

---

## vital_status

Description:
Patient survival status.

Business Importance:
Used in outcomes and survival analyses.
